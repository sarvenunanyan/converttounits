Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Who We Are

ConvertToUnits.net is a free online unit conversion tool. Our website address is https://converttounits.net.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate this website:

  • Usage data — pages visited, browser type, and referring URLs, collected anonymously via Google Analytics
  • Conversion inputs — values entered into converters are processed locally in your browser and are never sent to our servers
  • Cookies — used for analytics and advertising purposes

3. How We Use Data

  • To improve the content and functionality of our tools
  • To understand how visitors use our converters
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic. This collects anonymised data about how visitors interact with our site. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

5. Google AdSense

We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out at Google Ad Settings.

6. Cookies

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics usage tracking
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense ad serving

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@converttounits.net
